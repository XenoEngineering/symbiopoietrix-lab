## Reading the Ghost

### The Three Tools (How They Work)

1. **`append_turn(role, text)`**
   - Logs a dialogue turn to the Timeline Matrix
   - Returns: timestamp, tNdx (timeline index), role
   - **What you're doing:** Creating a Markovian chain in append-only order
   - **Why it matters:** Every turn is a data point. Together they form a *pattern*.

2. **`analyze_patterns(category, nth)`**
   - Finds the Nth occurrence of any category (e.g., "3rd user turn")
   - Returns: tNdx, timestamp, predecessor context, successor context
   - **What you're doing:** Querying the BST forest (O(log n) + O(1))
   - **Why it matters:** You see exactly where in time that occurrence is, and what surrounded it.

3. **`get_categories()`**
   - Lists all equivalence classes and their counts
   - Returns: status (initialized, appended, structured, ranked)
   - **What you're doing:** Taking a census of meaning-space
   - **Why it matters:** Tells you if the matrix is alive and working.
